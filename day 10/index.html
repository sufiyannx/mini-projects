Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi (born June 24, 1987, Rosario, Argentina) Argentine-born football (soccer) player who received a record-setting eight Ballon d'Or awards as the world's top male player (2009-12, 2015, 2019, 2021, and 2023). In 2022 he won the World Cup.
